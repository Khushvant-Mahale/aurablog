Who We Are

We are engineers, developers, and writers committed to the absolute convergence of beautiful front-end designs and stellar SEO visibility.

Founded in 2026, AuraBlog was created to bridge the gap between creative visual experiences and search engine algorithm requirements. Too often, gorgeous portfolios are hidden on page 5 of search rankings due to slow frameworks, bad metadata, or poor site architecture. Conversely, SEO-optimized sites often look dated and unengaging.

We prove that you can have it all. Our guides focus on using semantic elements, lightweight styling engines (like vanilla CSS), and smart page architectures that earn perfect PageSpeed scores while looking modern, fluid, and premium.
